I make games.

Really!

As the primary programmer and secondary game designer of a team named PixelFeet, I've released two games for the Ludum Dare Game Jam. You can check them out below!


Winter Is Coming . . . is a casual strategy game in which the player is tasked with growing the best tree he or she can before winter. Careful resource management between sunlight, water, and nutrients is the key to success in our first Ludum Dare submission. I'm also currently working on a post-compo version that includes more game modes, multiple trees, and HD graphics!

Starlight is a story-driven experience (with full voice acting by Nathan Kelleher) that places the player in the role of the Cosmonaut, a supernatural being capable of exploring and interacting with our solar system on a massive scale. As you play, you'll unravel the mystery behind Mars and follow the Cosmonaut's steep descent into madness.
